智慧視界 Intelligent Vision

在數位科技飛速發展的時代，人工智慧（AI）正在顛覆數位圖像的創作方式。
AI技術不再僅是輔助工具，而是主導了從構思到實現的整個過程，開啟了全新的視覺領域。

數位圖像創作不再依賴單純的靈感與技藝，AI透過算法生成複雜影像，模糊了手工與自動化的界限，並重新定義了數位圖像的可能性。
這場變革不僅是技術上的進步，更是對視覺文化的挑戰。面對AI驅動的新媒體時代，創作者們準備好迎接這場視覺革命了嗎？

Intelligent Vision

In this era of rapid digital technological advancement, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the way digital imagery is created. AI is no longer just a supporting tool but now leads the entire process from conception to realization, opening up a new realm of visual possibilities.

The creation of digital images no longer relies solely on inspiration and skill, as AI generates complex visuals through algorithms, blurring the boundaries between manual craftsmanship and automation, and redefining the potential of digital imagery.

This transformation is not merely a technological leap, but a challenge to visual culture. As we enter the AI-driven era of new media, are creators ready to embrace this visual revolution?

2D 轉 360：使用等距方格繪製《海綿寶寶》360 2D in 360: Using an Equirectangular Grid for SpongeBob 360

這場演講將深入探討如何將傳統2D動畫技術轉化為360度影片創作，分享《海綿寶寶》首部360度手繪動畫的製作流程與實際應用。

重點在於如何透過等距方格佈局，讓2D動畫師在熟悉的工作環境中，運用僅需一個背景、動畫圖層與簡單的VR輸出技術，輕鬆完成高品質的360度動畫。

這種低門檻的創作方式，為動畫專業人士提供了一個重新審視360度媒體製作的全新視角，並啟發更多可能性，適合任何想在技術與藝術間尋求平衡的專業工作者參與。

After directing and animating on the first Spongebob Squarepants 360 hand-animated film for Nickelodeon, I established a workflow for traditional 2D animators working in 360-film. Because traditional animators can work directly on an equirectangular layout, they can readily create an entire 360 film with nothing morethan a single background, animated layers, and a VR export.

This low-tech approach to a high-tech experience creates an access-point for those artists and 2D animators who would be offput by significant departures from a traditional animation production.

遊戲中的閒置時間 The Idle Time in Games

心流的概念出自Mihaly Csikszentmihaly的著作 Flow： The Psychology of Optimal Experience，書中將「心流」描述為全身心，未經打斷地關注於一件任務上。 這個概念經常被應用在遊戲設計理念裡。

完全理想的狀態下，心流自然是越流暢越好，但是在現實設計中，幾乎不可避免的會出現一段相對疲軟與緩和的區間，我把這段時間稱為Idle Time。

The concept of "Flow" originates from Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi's book Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience, in which "flow" is described as a state of being fully immersed and uninterruptedly focused on a task. This concept is often applied in game design principles.

Ideally, flow should be as seamless as possible. However, in practical design, it is almost inevitable to encounter a relatively sluggish or relaxed phase, which I refer to as "Idle Time."
